About

A little about my work

I'm a software developer based in Kampala, working across web development and applied research. Over the past few years I've delivered client-facing dashboards, internal reporting tools, and static frontend applications — usually working closely with finance and operations teams to turn messy requirements into dependable software.

I care about details that are easy to skip: documentation that a future teammate can actually follow, interfaces that stay usable on a patchy connection, and handovers that don't require a phone call two weeks later. Outside client work, I spend time studying financial technology platforms and how digital products build trust with new users.
